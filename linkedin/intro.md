## Profile
{id: profile}

It is like a CV

* Your Name - Capitalize your name properly
* Picture (professional, look in the eye)
* A title
* About section

* Language: English!
* Location: Country

* Describe your previous jobs.
* Use the proper keywords.
* Educational institutes
* Languages you speak
